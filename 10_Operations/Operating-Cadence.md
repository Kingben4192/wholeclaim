# WholeClaim Operating Cadence (the company OS)
Weekly rhythm (solo-founder scale, ~5 focused hrs):
Mon — Engineering review (Claude Code milestone check) · Tue — Marketing (schedule week's posts) · Wed — Content creation batch · Thu — Product (one Bible/Standard improvement) · Fri — Metrics pull · Sun — Founder review: 30 min, six numbers (Metrics Bible), ONE decision, logged in 00_Founder/Decisions.md.
Feature scoring — nothing ships because it's "cool." Score 1–5 each: Impact on north star · Difficulty · Revenue effect · Legal/compliance risk (inverse) · Support burden (inverse) · AI cost (inverse) · Documentation burden (inverse). Ship order = score order; any Legal risk score ≤2 requires counsel note first.
Hiring/Payroll/Vendors: empty by design until first hire/vendor; entries follow the Decision Log format.
