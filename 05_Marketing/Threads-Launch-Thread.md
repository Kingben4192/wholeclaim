4. The Adjuster Visit Script

## Posting Notes

- **Character counts:** Every post is under 500 characters including the X/12 tag. If Threads' counter flags 12/12, drop "That's 10." and keep the repost line.
- **Link post:** Paste the Gumroad URL on its own line so Threads renders the product card, same as the original thread. If you have multiple SKUs, use the flagship binder — one link, one decision.
- **Template discipline:** Every prompt keeps the proven skeleton — "Act as a [X] specialist" → first-person fear/pain → colon list of 4 deliverables → one "realistic" item to defuse skepticism.
- **Embedded case theory:** Prompts 7 and 9 carry your actual Grange arguments (supplement vs. new occurrence, loss-count math) as free value. That's your credibility moat — no generic prompt seller can write those.
- **Litigation safety:** The anchor references your real dispute without naming the carrier or case number. Keep it that way in replies too, and run the anchor by Monica Owens if you want a belt-and-suspenders check while the case is active.
- **The share mechanic:** The original earned a 49% share-to-like ratio because people send prompt lists to themselves. Your closer explicitly asks for the repost — the original never did. Small edge.
